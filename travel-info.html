Essential Travel Information for Taxila

Getting There

Located 35 km northwest of Islamabad, easily accessible by road. Regular transportation options available from major cities.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers mild temperatures and optimal conditions for exploring archaeological sites and outdoor attractions.

Practical Tips

Comfortable walking shoes, sun protection, and water are recommended. Guided tours provide comprehensive historical context.
